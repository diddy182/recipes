directions for a 9x13 cake.
• 1 16- ounce jar caramel sauce
3. When the cake is done baking use the round
• 1 8- ounce tub of Cool Whip
handle end of a wooden spoon and poke holes all
throughout the cake.
• 4 Heath bars (chopped)
4. Pour the sweetened condensed milk all over the
