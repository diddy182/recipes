How You Make A F**king Biscuit
Ingredients
• 2 cups (250 g) all-purpose flour, plus more for dusting
• 2 tablespoons baking powder
• ¼ teaspoon baking soda
• 1 teaspoon kosher salt
• ½ cup (115 g) very cold unsalted butter, diced, plus tablespoon, melted
• 1 cup (240 ml) buttermilk
Makes 8 Biscuits
Instructions
1. Preheat the oven to 450°F (230°C).
2. Sift flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a food processor bowl.
3. Add cold diced butter, pulse until pea-sized. Add buttermilk, pulse to form dough.
4. Dust flour on a surface, press dough into a ½ inch thick disk, fold 5 times, then press again.
5. Cut biscuits with a 3-inch cutter, place on baking sheet, spaced apart.
6. Bake 10-13 minutes until golden brown. Brush with melted butter.
Source: Yellowstone Recipe Book
