 Denise’s Sweet Potato Casserole
www.crunchycreamysweet.com
Prep: 15m Cook: 25m Total: 40m
Yield: 5 people
Family Holiday Side
Ingredients Instructions
• 2 lbs sweet potatoes 1. Butter 2 quart casserole dish. Set aside.
• 2 large eggs 2. Peel and dice sweet potatoes. Cook in a pot with
water until soft.
• 2 Tablespoons butter
3. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
• 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
4. Once potatoes are cooked, drain and mash. Add
• 1/2 teaspoon salt (divided)
butter, brown sugar, vanilla, salt and eggs. Mix
well. Using a whisk helps. Spread potato mixture
• 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
in prepared casserole dish. Set aside.
• 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
5. In another mixing bowl, mix together sugar, flour,
• 3 Tablespoons all-purpose flour salt and nuts. Add melted butter and stir with a
fork, until it forms crumbly dough.
• 2 Tablespoons butter melted (cooled)
6. Sprinkle the topping over the mashed potatoes.
• 1/4 cup chopped pecans
7. Bake casserole for 25 minutes. If desired, add
mini marshmallows. Place dish back in the oven
and bake until the marshmallows are toasted,
about 3 to 5 minutes.
8. Cool before serving.
