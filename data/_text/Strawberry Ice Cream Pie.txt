 Strawberry Ice Cream Pie

Prep: 10m Cook: 10m Total: 20m
Yield: 8,8 slices
Dessert treats
Ingredients Instructions
• 1 1/2 cups graham crackers, crushed 1. Preheat oven to 375ºF. Remove ice cream from
freezer and allow to soften.
• 1 tbsp brown sugar
2. In a food processor crush graham crackers until
• 3 tbsp butter, melted
fine. This will take about 12 graham crackers. Add
in the melted butter and brown sugar, pulse until
• 3 cups softened strawberry ice cream
a sand like mixture has formed.
 Add Items to Shopping List 3. Press the graham cracker crust evenly into the
bottom of a 9 inch pie pan, making sure to cover
the bottom and up the sides of the pan. Use the
back of a measuring cup or large spoon to press
the crust.
4. Bake the crust in the heated oven for 8-10
minutes or until golden brown. Remove from the
oven and place on a cooling rack. Cool for a few
minutes before adding the hot fudge and ice
cream.
5. With a spatula, spread the softened ice cream,
filling the pie pan to the top with the ice cream.
Cover with plastic wrap and place in the freezer
for at least 3 hours, or over night.
6. Store tightly covered in the freezer. Remove from
the freezer 5-10 minutes before serving for easier
slicing.
