on the surface and place each loaf into a greased
8x4 inch loaf pan tucked ends and seam side
down. Cover with a greased piece of plastic wrap
and let rise for about 45 minutes in a warm and
draft-free place until doubled in size.
5. Preheat oven to 390 degrees F and adjust oven
rack to lower-middle position.
6. Bake loaves for 25-30 minutes until golden brown
(and the loaf registers 208 to 210 degrees).
