Avocado Salad

A quick three-ingredient avocado salad with lime juice, fresh cilantro, and salt.

Yield2 servings
Prep10 mins
Total10 mins
Download PDF

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon minced fresh cilantro
  • Salt

Method

  1. Cut the avocado in half, take out the pit, and take off the skin. Chop the avocado and add it to a small bowl. Add the lime juice, cilantro, and salt. Gently mix.
