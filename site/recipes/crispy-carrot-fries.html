Crispy Carrot Fries + Sriracha Mayo

Oven-baked carrot fries coated in parmesan and seasonings until crispy, served with a sauce of choice.

Prep15 mins
Cook30 mins
Total45 mins
Download PDF

Ingredients

  • 1 lb carrots, peeled and cut into uniform fry shape
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • ⅓ cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Optional: parsley for garnish, sauce of choice for dipping

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, add the olive oil, parmesan, cornstarch, onion powder, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Mix well to combine.
  3. Add in the carrots and toss to coat, until all carrots are well coated.
  4. Spread the carrots evenly on the lined baking sheet. It's very important that they're spread in a single layer and not overlapping to allow them to get crispy.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes, flipping the carrots halfway.
  6. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve with a dipping sauce of choice (ketchup, ranch, pesto, or spicy mayo are recommended).
