Cucumber Salad

Thinly sliced cucumbers and red onion in a sweet-and-tangy white wine vinegar dressing, showered with fresh dill and chives — a cooling, refreshing summer side.

Yield6 servings
Prep10 mins
Total30 mins (incl. 20 min chill)
Download PDF

Ingredients

  • 2 English cucumbers, very thinly sliced
  • ½ large red onion, very thinly sliced
  • ¼ cup white wine vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon honey or agave nectar
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh dill
  • Chopped fresh chives, optional, for garnish
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. In a large bowl, toss together the cucumber, onion, vinegar, honey, and salt. Chill for 20 minutes.
  2. Transfer to a serving bowl, leaving any excess water behind. Sprinkle with the dill and chives, if desired. Season with several grinds of pepper and serve.

Notes

  • Less soggy: Chilling the dressed cucumbers for 20 minutes draws out their water — transfer to a clean bowl and leave the released liquid behind.
  • Regular cucumbers: If using regular cucumbers instead of English, peel them and scoop out the seeds before slicing.
  • Storage: Best the day it's made; leftovers keep in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 3 days.
