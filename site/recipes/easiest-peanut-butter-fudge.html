Easiest Peanut Butter Fudge Ever

A four-ingredient peanut butter fudge made on the stovetop with sugar, milk, peanut butter, and vanilla.

Yield20 servings
Total2 hrs 10 mins
Download PDF

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sugar
  • ½ cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ¾ cup creamy peanut butter

Method

  1. In a saucepan, slowly bring the milk and sugar to a boil over medium-low heat.
  2. Boil for exactly 2 and a half minutes, stirring constantly to prevent burning.
  3. Remove from heat, stir in the peanut butter and vanilla.
  4. Mix until smooth.
  5. Pour into a greased 8"x8" pan, and let cool before cutting. Enjoy!
