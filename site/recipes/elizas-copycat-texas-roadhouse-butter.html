Eliza's Copycat Texas Roadhouse Butter

A sweet whipped cinnamon-honey butter that tastes just like the one served at Texas Roadhouse — perfect on warm rolls.

Yield8 tablespoons
Prep5 mins
Total5 mins
Download PDF

Ingredients

  • 1 stick salted butter (at room temperature)
  • ¼ cup powdered sugar
  • 1½ tablespoons honey
  • ¾ teaspoon cinnamon

Method

  1. For a creamy consistency, ensure that your butter is very soft and has been at room temperature for at least an hour. Don't heat it in the microwave to achieve this as it will mess with the consistency of the butter. Mix all ingredients with an electric mixer until creamy and smooth. Refrigerate until ready to use and enjoy!
