Eliza's Christmas Puppy Chow

Festive holiday puppy chow: Rice Chex and pretzels coated in peanut butter and chocolate, dusted with powdered sugar, and studded with red and green M&Ms and mini peanut butter cups.

Yield24 servings
Prep15 mins
Total2 hrs 15 mins
Download PDF

Ingredients

  • 1½ cups semisweet or dark chocolate chips
  • 1½ cups creamy peanut butter
  • 5 cups Rice Chex cereal
  • 1½ cups mini pretzels (broken into pieces)
  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • 1½ cups red and green M&Ms
  • 1 cup mini peanut butter cups

Method

  1. In a large bowl, combine the Rice Chex cereal and pretzel pieces.
  2. Melt the peanut butter and chocolate together, either on the stovetop or in the microwave.
  3. Pour the peanut butter chocolate mixture over the cereal/pretzel mixture and stir to combine.
  4. Chill in the refrigerator until the mixture just barely begins to set up (about 30 minutes).
  5. Remove the mixture from the refrigerator.
  6. In a separate large bowl, put ½ cup powdered sugar on the bottom of the bowl. Add half of the chilled mixture, then add another ½ cup powdered sugar over the cereal and the remaining chocolate/cereal mixture.
  7. Gently stir until the powdered sugar is evenly distributed, tossing to coat the cereal with powdered sugar.
  8. Chill the mixture again in the refrigerator until it's set (about another 30-60 minutes).
  9. Add more powdered sugar, ¼ cup at a time, until the cereal/pretzel mixture is coated.
  10. Add the M&Ms and peanut butter cups and stir to combine.
  11. Transfer to a serving bowl and serve. Store in an airtight container at room temperature for 5-7 days.
