(NOT Fred Flintstone's) Fruity Pebble French Toast

Custard-soaked bread pressed into crushed Fruity Pebbles and pan-fried until crisp — a colorful, crunchy twist on classic French toast.

Yield8 slices
Prep10 mins
Cook10 mins
Total20 mins
Download PDF

Ingredients

  • 8 slices bread
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 3 cups Fruity Pebbles
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • ½ teaspoon cinnamon

Method

  1. In a shallow dish, whisk together eggs, milk, vanilla, and cinnamon until combined.
  2. In a separate bowl, add Fruity Pebbles. Crush the cereal to make it easier to stick to the bread.
  3. Working on 1 slice at a time, dip bread until fully soaked, about 45 seconds.
  4. Transfer soaked bread to the Fruity Pebbles and press to coat. Use your hands if necessary to get the cereal to stick.
  5. In a large skillet over medium-low heat, melt 2 tablespoons of butter. Add French toast and cook about 3 minutes each side.
  6. Repeat with remaining bread, adding butter if needed.

Notes

  • Tip: Pour ½ cup of cereal into the milk mixture and let it sit for a few minutes to sweeten up the milk before dipping the bread.
