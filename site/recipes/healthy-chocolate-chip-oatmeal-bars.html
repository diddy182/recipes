Healthy Chocolate Chip Oatmeal Bars

Naturally sweetened oat flour bars with maple syrup, coconut oil, and chocolate chips — an easy gluten-free treat.

Yield12 servings
Prep5 mins
Cook20 mins
Total25 mins
Calories179 kcal
Download PDF

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • ⅓ cup almond milk, unsweetened
  • ⅓ cup pure maple syrup
  • ½ cup melted coconut oil
  • 1½ teaspoons vanilla extract
  • ¼ cup coconut sugar
  • 1¼ cups homemade oat flour
  • ⅔ cup rolled oats, gluten-free or regular
  • ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon cinnamon
  • ½ cup chocolate chips

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Line or grease an 8x8 pan with parchment and set aside.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the eggs, almond milk, maple syrup, melted coconut oil, vanilla extract, and coconut sugar.
  3. Add in the oat flour, rolled oats, baking powder, and cinnamon. Mix and gently fold in the chocolate chips.
  4. Evenly pour the batter into the lined pan and sprinkle extra chocolate chips on top.
  5. Bake for 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean and the bars are golden brown.
  6. Let cool before cutting into bars and enjoy!

Notes

  • Homemade Oat Flour: Blend rolled oats in a high-speed blender until it resembles a flour-like consistency.
  • Almond milk can be substituted with another nut milk or regular milk. Coconut sugar can be substituted 1:1 with light brown sugar.
