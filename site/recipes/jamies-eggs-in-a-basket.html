Jamie's Eggs in a Basket

Buttered bread with a circle cut from the center, fried with an egg cracked into the hole and the cut-out round fried alongside. Finished with butter and maple syrup.

Yield1 serving
Prep5 mins
Cook5 mins
Total10 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 slice white or wheat bread
  • 1 egg
  • Butter
  • Maple syrup, for serving

Method

  1. Butter the bread. Using a small round cutter (or a cup) that fits within the bread, cut out a circle from the center.
  2. Place both the bread and the separated buttered circle in a frying pan.
  3. Crack an egg into the middle of the hole in the bread.
  4. Fry until the egg is mostly set on the bottom, then flip the bread. Flip the buttered circle too.
  5. Top with more butter and maple syrup.
