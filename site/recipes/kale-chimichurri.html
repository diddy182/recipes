Kale Chimichurri

A fresh, garlicky blender sauce of kale, green herbs, olive oil, lime, nuts or seeds, and a punch of red pepper flakes.

Yield1¼ cups (5 servings of ¼ cup each)
Total5 mins
Calories235 kcal
Download PDF

Ingredients

  • 1 cup packed kale (stems removed)
  • 1 cup packed cilantro and/or parsley
  • ½ cup olive oil
  • ¼ cup water
  • Juice of 2 limes (or ¼ cup white vinegar)
  • ¼ cup cashews, sunflower seeds, or almonds
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 teaspoon coarse salt
  • ½ teaspoon red pepper flakes

Method

  1. Blend all the sauce ingredients together in a small blender or food processor until mostly smooth.
  2. Pour on everything and feel like a kale-loaded million bucks.

Notes

  • Vegan and gluten-free with no adaptation needed. Not freezable. Keeps in the fridge about 5 days.
