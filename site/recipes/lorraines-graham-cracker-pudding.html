Lorraine's Memere's Graham Cracker Pudding

A no-bake layered icebox dessert of graham crackers, vanilla and chocolate pudding, peanut butter, and Cool Whip, chilled until the crackers soften.

Yield8 servings
Prep1 hr
Total1 hr
Download PDF

Ingredients

  • 1 box graham crackers
  • 2 boxes vanilla pudding (large, 5.1 oz each)
  • 1 box chocolate pudding (small, 3.9 oz)
  • 1 jar peanut butter
  • 1 package softened Cool Whip (8 oz)

Method

  1. Prepare the puddings according to package instructions. Prepare the vanilla pudding and set aside.
  2. Prepare the chocolate pudding.
  3. Spread peanut butter on the graham crackers. Place the first layer of graham cracker/peanut butter on the bottom of a 9x13 pan (peanut butter side up).
  4. Spread ⅓ of the vanilla pudding on top of that.
  5. Spread a layer of Cool Whip (works best if soft) on top of the vanilla pudding.
  6. Continue layering in order as above.
  7. Layer until there's just enough room to spread chocolate pudding on top as a finish (use chocolate pudding to top the dessert only).
  8. Crush the remaining graham crackers. Sprinkle them over the chocolate pudding for garnish.
  9. Refrigerate 1-2 hours or until the graham crackers are soft.

Notes

  • Memere doesn't add peanut butter.
  • You can use any flavor graham cracker and any flavor pudding you wish. Cinnamon crackers with cream cheese pudding is great.
  • You can also make the puddings from scratch if you prefer, but that requires more effort and time — this is the quick version.
