Breakfast

Memere's Crepes

A simple family crepe batter of milk, flour, eggs, and a touch of sugar and vanilla, cooked in a frying pan.

Download PDF

Ingredients

  • 1½ cups milk
  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla

Method

  1. Mix all ingredients together.
  2. Cook in a frying pan.
