Peanut Butter Cookies

Three-ingredient flourless peanut butter cookies with the classic criss-cross fork pattern.

Yield9 servings
Prep10 mins
Cook10 mins
Total20 mins
Download PDF

Ingredients

  • 1 cup peanut butter (no sugar added)
  • ¾ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Line two baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. Mix peanut butter, sugar, and egg together in a bowl using an electric mixer or spoon, until smooth and creamy. Roll mixture into small balls and arrange on a baking sheet; flatten each with the back of a fork, making a criss-cross pattern.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven for 10 minutes. Cool cookies on the baking sheet for 2 minutes before moving to a cooling rack for a few more minutes.
