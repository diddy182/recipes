Steve's Bacon Wrapped Asparagus

Asparagus spears wrapped in thick-cut bacon and roasted with a honey-dijon-soy glaze, then broiled until crisp.

Yield6 servings
Prep15 mins
Cook20 mins
Total35 mins
Download PDF

Ingredients

  • 1 bunch asparagus
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • ½ teaspoon cracked peppercorn
  • ½ teaspoon sea salt
  • 8 pieces thick center-cut bacon
  • 2 teaspoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F.
  2. Wash and dry the asparagus spears. Make a cut from the bottom of the spear and discard. Add the asparagus to a mixing bowl and toss with the olive oil, peppercorn, and sea salt.
  3. Cut the bacon down the center. Take a strip of bacon and, starting at the top of the asparagus spear, wrap the bacon around the stalk. Tuck the end of the bacon in, or use a toothpick to secure it.
  4. In a small mixing bowl, combine the honey, dijon mustard, soy sauce, red pepper flakes, and minced garlic. Whisk well.
  5. Lay the asparagus onto a wire rack-lined baking sheet. Brush each with the glaze.
  6. Place the baking sheet into the oven on the center rack. Bake for 7 minutes, then remove from the oven. Turn the asparagus over and brush with the glaze. Place back into the oven for 7 more minutes. Turn off the oven.
  7. Remove the baking sheet from the oven and brush the asparagus with the glaze on both sides. Place under the broiler for 4 minutes, turning halfway through. This will crisp up the bacon.
