Susan's Bread Pudding with Vanilla Sauce

A cozy baked bread pudding spiced with cinnamon and nutmeg, served warm under a rich brown sugar vanilla cream sauce.

Yield8 servings
Prep20 mins
Total1 hr 15 mins
Download PDF

Ingredients

Bread Pudding

  • 2½ cups unseasoned toasted bread cubes (from 12-oz. pkg.)
  • 1½ cups milk
  • ¼ cup firmly packed brown sugar
  • ½ teaspoon cinnamon
  • ½ teaspoon nutmeg
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla
  • 2 eggs, beaten

Vanilla Sauce

  • ⅓ cup sugar
  • ⅓ cup firmly packed brown sugar
  • ⅓ cup whipping cream
  • ⅓ cup butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla

Method

  1. Heat oven to 350°F. Grease a 1-quart casserole. In the greased casserole, combine the bread cubes and milk; mix well.
  2. In a medium bowl, combine ¼ cup brown sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, ½ teaspoon vanilla, and eggs; blend well. Add the egg mixture to the bread cube mixture; mix well.
  3. Bake at 350°F for 45 to 50 minutes or until the pudding is set.
  4. In a small saucepan, combine the sugar, ⅓ cup brown sugar, whipping cream, and butter; mix well. Cook over medium heat for about 5 minutes or until the mixture comes to a boil, stirring occasionally. Stir in 1 teaspoon vanilla. Serve the warm sauce over the warm pudding.
