Trader Joe's Copycat Curried Chicken

A creamy curried chicken salad with Greek yogurt, carrots, raisins, and almonds — a copycat of the Trader Joe's deli favorite.

Yield4 servings
Prep5 mins
Cook5 mins
Total10 mins
Download PDF

Ingredients

  • ½ tablespoon honey
  • 1 cup nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons light mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons good quality curry powder, more or less to taste
  • 1 lb raw chicken breast, poached and cubed
  • ½ cup shredded carrots
  • ½ cup raisins
  • 2 scallions, chopped, whites discarded
  • 1 stalk celery, minced
  • ¼ cup sliced almonds
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste (optional)

Method

  1. Whisk together honey, yogurt, mayonnaise, and curry powder in a small bowl and set aside. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
  2. Combine remaining ingredients in a large bowl and stir in desired amount of dressing.
  3. Enjoy!
